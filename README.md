This model is designed as a practice tool for candidates preparing for investment appraisal assessments. It simulates a long‑term partnership between an investment company and an operational delivery partner responsible for running a portfolio of leisure assets.

The model helps candidates understand how to:

Analyse long‑term operational contracts

Evaluate commercial structures such as management fees, surplus share, and gainshare

Assess risk transfer between an investor and an operator

Interpret DBOM‑style responsibilities (Design, Build, Operate, Maintain)

Form a high‑level investment recommendation based on limited information

It is structured to mirror the type of appraisal task commonly used in investment analyst, Senior FP&A Analysit and Finance Business Partnering roles.
